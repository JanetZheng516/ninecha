What is Ninecha

"Ninecha" is the similar pronunciation of "milk tea" in Chinese. We hope that while offering popular Asian drinks, making a connection between the local peopel and Asian culture. Our name is the first step.

"Nine" has the same pronunciation as "PERMANENT" in Chinese. Apart from being a milk tea shop, we hope to be with you all the time -- 9 Forever.

For providing the most authentic boba milk tea (also called pearl milk tea), our pearls and tea are all imported directly from Taiwan, and strictly follow the making process.
